Donations

Thank you for your interest in donating money to the Translate.Net project.

One-time donation

If you would like to donate money to the Translate.Net project, please select a currency and amount and then click the AlertPay icon. You will be taken to the AlertPay website where you will be able to donate some amount to the Translate.Net project. If you have any questions concerning donations, please email me at translate.net@gmail.com. Please make sure you have read the legal notice at the bottom of this page.

Regular contribution each month

If you would like to donate money to the Translate.Net project each month with your AlertPay subscription, please select a currency and amount and then click the AlertPay icon. You will be taken to the AlertPay website where you will be able to initiate the monthly donation. Your credit card will be automatically charged each month via AlertPay until you cancel this monthly donation. If you have any questions concerning donations, please email me at translate.net@gmail.com. Please make sure you have read the legal notice at the bottom of this page.
